About the Authors

Electrogenesis Project
The birth of the electronic age

"Electrogenesis" is an interactive educational platform that documents the fundamental milestones of electronic evolution between 1886 and 1924, a crucial period that established the technological foundations of the modern world.
This project represents the collaborative effort of a multidisciplinary team dedicated to the preservation and digital presentation of the history of electronics, combining historical rigor with technical excellence.
